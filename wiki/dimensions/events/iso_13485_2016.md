# ISO 13485: 2016

## Overview
[[ISO 13485: 2016]] is the definitive international standard for quality management systems (QMS) specifically tailored for the medical device industry. It provides the foundational requirements for any organization involved in the design, development, manufacture, and distribution of medical devices.

## Description
This standard serves as the global benchmark for ensuring that medical devices are consistently safe, effective, and meet high standards of quality. Adherence to [[ISO 13485: 2016]] is mandatory for demonstrating regulatory compliance and a commitment to high-quality patient care. It functions as the core international standard for quality management systems within the medical device sector.
